Grilled Chicken


Smoky Grilled chicken to satisfy all your cravings!
Learn the secret to cooking an amazing grilled chicken!!!

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 c.balsamic vinegar
  • 3 tbsp.extra-virgin olive oil
  • tbsp.brown sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp.dried thyme
  • 1 tsp.dried rosemary
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Freshly chopped parsley, for garnish
  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together balsamic vinegar, olive oil, brown sugar, garlic, and dried herbs, and season generously with salt and pepper. Reserve Â¼ cup.

  2. Add chicken to the bowl and toss to combine. Let marinate at least 20 minutes and up to overnight.

  3. Preheat grill to medium high. Add chicken and grill, basting with reserved marinade, until cooked through, 6 minutes per side.

  4. Garnish with parsley before serving.
